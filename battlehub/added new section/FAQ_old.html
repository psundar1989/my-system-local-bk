FAQ's

What Is GameBattles Premium?

GameBattles Premium allows you to take your competitive experience to the next level. Premium members gain access to additional features such as superior match support, free access to Premium Tournaments, discounts, and much more!

What are credits?

How do I change my password?

Your password can be changed in your account settings page.

How do I submit a ticket?

You can submit a ticket to GameBattles support by hovering over your username on the navigation and selecting “Tickets & Escalations”. You may also submit a ticket here.

How does someone get banned?

Bans are issued when someone has violated GameBattles Rules. Ban lengths and punishments are determined based on the severity of the offense. In addition to being banned on GameBattles, accounts that have broken GameBattles rules are also subject to having their GB rank and overall record reset. Once banned, a player has the option to contest their ban. Once contested, a senior staff member will review the ban and verify the ban has been placed properly.
